Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

SwimAnalytics is designed with privacy at its core. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Don't Collect

SwimAnalytics is a privacy-first application. We want to be clear about what we don't do:

  • No Account Registration: You never need to create an account or provide an email address
  • No Personal Data Collection: We don't collect names, emails, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We don't use third-party analytics services to track your behavior
  • No Data Servers: We don't operate any servers that store your workout data
  • No Sharing: We have nothing to share because we collect nothing

2. How SwimAnalytics Works

2.1 Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Core Data: Your workout history and analytics are stored in a local database on your iPhone
  • UserDefaults: App preferences and trial information are stored locally
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it

2.2 HealthKit Integration

SwimAnalytics integrates with Apple HealthKit to read your swimming workouts:

  • You must explicitly grant permission to access HealthKit data
  • We only read swimming workout data that you choose to sync
  • HealthKit data is processed locally and never sent to external servers
  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings

2.3 Subscription Management

SwimAnalytics offers in-app subscriptions managed by Apple:

  • Apple handles all payments: Your payment information is processed by Apple, not by us
  • No payment data access: We never see or store your credit card or payment details
  • Trial tracking: The 7-day free trial is tracked locally on your device using the first launch date
  • Subscription verification: We verify your subscription status through Apple's StoreKit framework (local verification only)

3. Data You Generate

3.1 Workout Analytics

When you sync workouts from HealthKit, SwimAnalytics calculates:

  • Swimming pace and distance metrics
  • Critical Swim Speed (CSS)
  • Training Stress Score (TSS)
  • Training load (CTL/ATL/TSB)
  • Personal records and training zones

All calculations are performed locally on your device. This data is stored in your device's local database and is never transmitted to external servers.

3.2 Data Export

You can export your workout data in multiple formats (JSON, CSV, HTML, PDF):

  • Exports are generated locally on your device
  • You control where exported files are saved (Files app, email, etc.)
  • We don't receive copies of your exported data

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Apple Services

SwimAnalytics uses Apple's built-in frameworks:

  • HealthKit: For reading swimming workout data (Apple's privacy policy applies)
  • StoreKit: For managing subscriptions (Apple's privacy policy applies)
  • App Store: For app distribution and updates (Apple's privacy policy applies)

4.2 No Third-Party Analytics

We deliberately chose not to integrate:

  • Google Analytics
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Facebook SDK
  • Crashlytics or error reporting services
  • Any advertising networks or trackers

5. Children's Privacy

SwimAnalytics does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

6. Data Security

While we don't collect or transmit data, your local data is protected by:

  • iOS Encryption: Your device's built-in encryption protects all local data
  • App Sandbox: iOS isolates SwimAnalytics from other apps
  • Secure Storage: Core Data and UserDefaults are stored in your app's protected container

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Export: Export your data anytime in multiple formats
  • Delete: Delete the app to remove all local data
  • HealthKit Permissions: Revoke access in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Providing an in-app notice for material changes

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Legal Basis (GDPR Compliance)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA):

  • We don't process any personal data, so GDPR data processing requirements don't apply
  • No consent is required for data collection because we don't collect data
  • You have no data to request, correct, or delete because we store nothing externally

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

For California residents:

  • We don't sell personal information because we don't collect it
  • We don't share personal information with third parties
  • You have nothing to request, delete, or opt-out of because we collect nothing
