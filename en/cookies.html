Cookie Policy

Last updated:

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Cookies

At onmedic we use cookies for:

  • Functionality: Remember your preferences (language, settings)
  • Analytics: Understand how you use our website
  • Security: Protect you against malicious activities
  • Continuous improvement: Identify areas for improvement on the site

Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration Necessary
Technical Enable basic website navigation Session Yes
Preferences Remember your user preferences 1 year No
Analytics Help us understand website usage 2 years No
Security Protect against attacks and fraud 24 hours Yes

Third-Party Cookies

We use some third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Fonts: To load typography
  • Formspree: To process contact forms
  • Hosting services: For site security and performance

How to Manage Cookies

From our website

You can manage your cookie preferences from the banner that appears at the bottom of the screen or by clicking here.

From your browser

You can also control cookies from your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Site permissions > Cookies

Consequences of Disabling Cookies

If you disable all cookies:

  • Some website elements may not work properly
  • You'll need to re-enter your preferences each visit
  • We won't be able to remember your cookie consent
  • The browsing experience may be less smooth

Updates to this Policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in cookie usage. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
