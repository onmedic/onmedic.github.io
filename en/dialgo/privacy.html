Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 22, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Dialgo is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, share, or use your personal data for any purpose other than providing you with our family communication service.

1. Information We Collect

Dialgo collects the following information to provide our family messaging service:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you sign in with Google or Apple
  • Family Data: Family group information, member relationships, and roles
  • Messages: Text messages and audio messages sent within your family group
  • Health Management Data: Medication schedules and medical visit information (if you choose to use these features)
  • Device Tokens: For push notification delivery

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Enable communication between family members
  • Deliver push notifications for new messages and reminders
  • Provide medication and medical visit management features
  • Maintain and improve the app's functionality

What We Do NOT Do:

  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT use your data for marketing purposes
  • We do NOT analyze your messages for any purpose
  • We do NOT create user profiles for advertising

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services provided by Google:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access is restricted to authenticated family members only
  • We implement industry-standard security practices
  • Data is stored in secure data centers

4. Data Sharing

We share your data only with:

  • Your Family Members: Messages and family data are shared within your family group
  • Service Providers: Firebase (Google) for backend infrastructure and RevenueCat for subscription management

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

5. Subscription Information

If you subscribe to Dialgo Premium:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store)
  • We never see or store your payment card details
  • We only receive confirmation of your subscription status

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data
  • Correct: Update your profile information at any time

To exercise these rights, use the settings within the app or contact us directly.

7. Children's Privacy

Dialgo is designed as a family communication app. Children under 13 should only use the app under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Your personal data is deleted from our systems
  • Messages within family groups may be retained for other family members
  • Some data may be retained in backups for a limited period

9. International Users

Dialgo is operated from Spain. Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

10. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Economic Area:

  • We process data based on your consent and contractual necessity
  • You have the right to data portability
  • You can withdraw consent at any time
  • You can lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
